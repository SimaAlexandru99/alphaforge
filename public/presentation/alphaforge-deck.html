Why AlphaForge?

Transparency Agents touching capital need auditable, explainable actions — not black-box signals.
Risk control Hard guardrails enforced at every step before any order reaches the exchange.
End-to-end loop Market data → decision → risk gate → execution → persistence → attestation.
Hackathon fit Kraken CLI execution path; optional ERC-8004 / Sepolia trust layer.

Intelligence layer

AlphaForge · 05 / 10

Risk gate · lib/agent/risk.ts

  • Max exposure — position size limits vs portfolio
  • Stop-loss / take-profit thresholds
  • Duplicate guard — no duplicate open position per symbol
  • Paper mode — safe defaults for hackathon demos

No execution without passing the risk gate — models suggest, policy enforces.
